Atr-i is a specialized perfume platform dedicated to helping you discover the finest luxury fragrances and comparing prices across the best trusted stores.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the perfume shopping experience. We provide detailed information about fragrance notes, longevity, and sillage, along with real-time price comparisons so you can find your perfect scent at the best value.

What We Cover

  • Fragrance Reviews Honest feedback on longevity and sillage for men, women, and niche perfumes.
  • Price Comparison We track prices from major retailers like Amazon, Sephora, and Noon.
  • Scent Discovery A complete guide to perfume pyramids (Top, Heart, and Base notes).

Our Core Values

  • Accuracy in fragrance notes and components
  • Helping users find the best market value
  • Transparency in affiliate recommendations

Our Vision

We aim to become the leading digital destination for perfume lovers globally, providing the most comprehensive fragrance database and unbeatable price insights.
